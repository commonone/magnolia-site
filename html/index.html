Publish iPad magazines quickly and easily with Maggie™ 

Newsstand.

Leverage the power of Apple's Newsstand. Maggy lets you choose to distribute your titles through Newsstand so readers find your title quickly, and notifies them when new content is available.

Sell Content.

Maggy lets you monetize your new or existing creative by offering in-app purchases. Issues can also be sold for free—it's up to you.

Feature-Rich.

Embedded videos, slideshows, popups, animations—Maggy lets you deliver rich, engaging user experiences with your content.

Notifications.

Alert your users using native push notifications when new content or titles are available.

Insights.

Gain valuable insights into your content's performance by tracking every user interaction within your Maggy app. Provide us with your Google Analytics or Flurry keys and integrate the analytics directly into your company's dashboard.
